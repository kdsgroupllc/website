FAQ

Frequently asked questions

What is the minimum order quantity?

Our minimums are among the lowest in the industry. You can start with a small batch rather than a five-figure inventory commitment. Exact minimums depend on the product and packaging, and we'll confirm them during scoping.

What is the typical lead time?

Most projects ship within 2–3 weeks of approval. Timelines vary with formulation complexity, packaging and component availability. We provide a firm date once your specification is confirmed.

Do you offer custom formulation or only white label?

Both. You can launch quickly with a proven white-label product or develop a fully custom formulation with our guidance, whichever best fits your brand, timeline and budget.

Can I supply my own packaging and labels?

Yes. Provide print-ready artwork and packaging, or we'll connect you with trusted design partners. We review files before anything goes to print.

Do you help with compliance and labeling guidance?

We provide compliance guidance during formula confirmation and label prep. Product-specific testing, COAs and documentation requirements are defined during project scoping.

Can you scale to larger production runs later?

Absolutely. Many clients start small and scale to thousands of units on the same line, with the same specifications and quality processes throughout.
